Community Bulletin

City Updates & Summer Programs

The city council is pleased to announce the grand opening of Riverside Park this Saturday morning. The park features over five miles of walking trails, a state-of-the-art playground, and covered picnic areas. Residents are invited to attend the ribbon-cutting ceremony at 10 AM near the main entrance. Local food trucks and community vendors will be available throughout the day.

This project has been in development for over two years and represents a significant investment in community green spaces. We hope to see you there! Additionally, the summer recreation program schedule has been officially released. Registration opens next Monday for swimming lessons, tennis camps, and youth soccer leagues. Early registration is strongly recommended as spots fill quickly. Need-based scholarships are available for qualifying families.
